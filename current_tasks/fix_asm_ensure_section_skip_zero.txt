Fix: assembler SkipExpr and Zero directives should auto-create .text section

Problem: When assembly files have .skip or .zero directives before any explicit
.section directive, the assembler errors with "no active section for .skip".
GAS implicitly creates a .text section in this case.

This breaks the x86 defconfig kernel build on arch/x86/lib/clear_page_64.S
where SYM_FUNC_START expands to `.balign 16, 0x90;; .skip 16, 0x90;` before
any section directive.

Fix: Add self.ensure_section()? calls to the SkipExpr and Zero handlers in
elf_writer_common.rs, matching what's already done for Label.
