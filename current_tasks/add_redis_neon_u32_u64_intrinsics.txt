Add missing ARM NEON u32/u64 intrinsics needed by Redis on ARM

Missing intrinsics (all appearing as undefined references in Redis ARM build):
- vaddq_u64: 64-bit lane vector add
- vextq_u64: Extract/concat-shift for u64
- vget_high_u32: Get high half of uint32x4_t -> uint32x2_t
- vget_low_u32: Get low half of uint32x4_t -> uint32x2_t
- vmlal_u32: Widening multiply-accumulate u32
- vmovn_u64: Narrow u64 to u32
- vreinterpretq_u32_u64: Reinterpret cast u64x2 -> u32x4
- vshrn_n_u64: Shift right narrow u64 to u32
- vuzpq_u32: Unzip/deinterleave u32
- Also: vdup_n_u32, vcombine_u32, vaddq_u32, vcreate_u32, vreinterpretq_u64_u32
