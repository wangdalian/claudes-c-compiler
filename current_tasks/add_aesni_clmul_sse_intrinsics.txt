Add AES-NI, CLMUL, and additional SSE intrinsics needed by mbedtls

The mbedtls selftest crashes on x86 because it uses AES-NI and CLMUL intrinsics
(via immintrin.h/wmmintrin.h) that the compiler doesn't support.

Intrinsics to add:
- _mm_clmulepi64_si128 (PCLMULQDQ)
- _mm_aesenc_si128, _mm_aesenclast_si128 (AES encrypt)
- _mm_aesdec_si128, _mm_aesdeclast_si128 (AES decrypt)
- _mm_aesimc_si128 (AES inverse mix columns)
- _mm_aeskeygenassist_si128 (AESKEYGENASSIST with imm8)
- _mm_slli_si128 / _mm_srli_si128 (byte shift: PSLLDQ/PSRLDQ)
- _mm_slli_epi64 / _mm_srli_epi64 (bit shift: PSLLQ/PSRLQ)
- _mm_shuffle_epi32 (PSHUFD with imm8)
- _mm_loadl_epi64 (MOVQ load low 64 bits)

Also need to provide our own wmmintrin.h and immintrin.h headers.
