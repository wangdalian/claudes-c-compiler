Unifying x86 and i686 assembler instruction coverage.

Adding missing 32-bit compatible instructions to the i686 encoder that exist in x86,
and adding missing instructions from i686 to x86. Focus areas:
- Flag manipulation (sahf, lahf, cld, std, clc, stc, cmc, pushf, popf, etc.)
- SSE packed float arithmetic (addpd/subpd/mulpd/divpd, addps/subps/mulps/divps)
- SSSE3 (palignr, pshufb, phaddw, phaddd, phsubw, phsubd, pmulhrsw)
- SSE4.1 (roundsd/ss/pd/ps, blend*, ptest, pmin*/pmax*, pinsrd/pextrd/pinsrb/pextrb, pmovzx*/pmovsx*)
- SSE3 data movement (movapd, movhlps, movlhps, movddup, movshdup, movsldup, movntps, movntpd)
- SSE3 horizontal (haddpd/hsubpd/haddps/hsubps, addsubpd/addsubps)
- Prefetch instructions
- Rotate through carry (rcl/rcr)
- leave, cmpsd/cmpss/cmppd/cmpps SSE, comisd/comiss
- x87 gaps (fxtract, fadd/fmul/fsub/fdiv reg-reg, fnstenv/fldenv, etc.)
- Adding comisd/comiss/cmpsd-SSE/cmpss/cmppd/cmpps/ftst/fxam/fucomi/movsbw/movzbw/pcmpgtd/pmulhuw/rdmsr/wrmsr to x86
